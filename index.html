01 — Who I Am

Beyond the
developer label

After 10+ years building web products — from fintech platforms in West Africa to municipal portals in Canada — I've learned something most developers don't talk about: the code is never the hard part.

The hard part is understanding what a business actually needs, designing the right architecture to get there, and delivering something that works — without hand-holding or micromanagement.

I work as a technical partner, not a contractor waiting for tickets. I bring ideas, challenge assumptions when needed, and build with an eye on long-term maintainability. When a project calls for it, I lead and coordinate a team.

Based in Cotonou, Bénin. Available to clients in Europe, Canada, the Americas, and across Africa.
